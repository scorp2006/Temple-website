# Sri Jagajjanani — Homepage design spec (from Figma "1920w light", file BlvEWzdg1Nz9Z6u1Jnazy6)

Captured from the authoritative final design. Match this layout.

## Overall
- **Light/cream site**, NOT a dark maroon hero. Red (#CF0038-ish) + maroon + gold accents on cream.
- Brand: "Sri Jagajjanani Devasthanam". Footer credit "Developed by AJS Innovations", "© 2022".
- Tagline (Telugu, baked into hero image): "ప్రపంచములోనే 2వ శ్రీ జగజ్జననీ దేవాలయము, నంద్యాల జిల్లా".

## Header (light, sticky)
- Left: logo + "Sri Jagajjanani Devasthanam" (two-line)
- Nav: Home · Sevas & Darshanam · Donations · Online Booking · Media Room · Support
- Right: language switcher · search icon · small social/profile icons
- Thin top utility bar above main header (very thin, dark) optional.

## Hero
- Full-bleed banner IMAGE (the Telugu deity+lion+gopuram art = /images/hero-banner.jpg). Text is in the image; no overlay heading needed. Full width, ~600-700px tall on desktop.

## Latest News ticker
- Thin dark/maroon strip directly under hero: "Latest News" label + scrolling marquee text + a button on the right.

## About section
- Heading: small "About" eyebrow + "Sri Jagajjanani Ammavaru" (red, serif).
- Left: body paragraphs + "Read More" button.
- Right: deity line-art illustration (/images/deity-lineart.png) as a light watermark/graphic.

## Service cards (row of 4)
Each card = vertical gradient tile (light red → deep red), white text, icon at top, title, description, button:
1. e-Hundi — "Donate Now"
2. Annadanam — "Donate Now"
3. Gosala — "More Info"
4. Sevas & Darshanam — "Book Now"
(Lion line-art faint behind the row.)

## Jagajjanani TV
- Maroon gradient band. Left: logo + "Jagajjanani TV" heading + description ("24×7 webcast devotional channel in Telugu, launched October 2022, by Sri Jagajjanani Devasthanam") + "Watch Now" button.
- Right: large video embed (black box / YouTube).

## Photo Gallery
- Heading "Photo Gallery" + subtitle "The Beauty of Sacred Sri Jagajjanani Devasthanam" + "View All" button.
- Mosaic grid of temple photos (one tall left, rest in a 2-row grid). Use /images/* photos.

## Two CTA panels
- Side by side, ornamental corner brackets:
  - Cream panel "Seva & Darshanam" + "View" button
  - Red panel "Main Offerings" + "View" button

## Footer (cream, rich)
- Scalloped/decorative top border.
- 6 columns: About · Sevas & Darshanam · Donations · Online Booking · Media Room · Support (each w/ sublinks).
  - About: The Goddess, The Temple, The Mahatyam, The Nirmaanam
  - Donations: Overview, e-Hundi, AnnaPrasadam Trust, GoSamrakshana Trust
  - Online Booking: Overview, Seva Booking, Darshanam Tickets, Donations

## Palette (from provided swatches)
- Reds: #FFFFFF #FFDCDB #FBBAB7 #F49795 #EA7274 #DE4A55 #CF0038
- Plus maroon/burgundy + turmeric gold accents.
- NOTE: user wants a MATTE finish — so soften the brightest reds slightly, keep surfaces calm/cream, avoid glossy gradients (use subtle ones).
